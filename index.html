About Oriental Symbol Tattoos

Oriental symbols have been used in tattoo art for decades, but choosing the right symbol requires understanding its true meaning and cultural context. A poorly chosen symbol can lead to embarrassment or unintended meanings.

We're here to help you make an informed choice. Each of our designs comes with:

  • Historical context: Where does this symbol come from?
  • Multiple meanings: What are all the possible interpretations?
  • Usage examples: How is this symbol used in modern Eastern culture?
  • Common mistakes: What should you avoid?
